[RAG Document 1]: SOP-101 (IV Kits): In the event of an IV Kit shortage in the Emergency Department (ED), substitute with Pediatric IV Kits (if patient < 40kg) or use Central Line Kits for critical patients. Do not use expired IV Kits under any circumstances.
[RAG Document 2]: SOP-102 (IV Kits): ICU protocol for IV Kit shortage requires immediately halting elective surgeries and transferring all available IV Kits to the ICU. Contact Central Supply for emergency redistribution.
