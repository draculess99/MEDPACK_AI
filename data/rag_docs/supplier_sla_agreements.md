## SLA-001: Medline Industries — PPE and General Medical Supplies

**Supplier:** Medline Industries  
**Contract Period:** January 2025 – December 2025  
**Account Manager:** medline-rep@medline.example.com

### Standard Delivery

| Category | Standard Lead Time | Emergency Lead Time |
|---|---|---|
| Surgical Masks (Level 1, 3) | 2 business days | 4 hours |
| N95 Respirators | 2 business days | 4 hours |
| Isolation Gowns | 2 business days | 4 hours |
| Exam Gloves (Nitrile, Latex) | 3 business days | 8 hours |
| Wound Care Packs | 3 business days | 12 hours |

### Emergency Order Terms

- Emergency delivery is available 24/7 including weekends and holidays.
- A **$500 premium** applies to all emergency orders placed on weekends.
- A **$250 premium** applies to all emergency orders placed on weekday evenings (after 6 PM).
- Minimum emergency order: 1 case (varies by product).

### Performance Guarantees

- Standard orders delivered on time: **≥ 95%** of the time.
- Emergency orders delivered within stated window: **≥ 98%** of the time.
- Penalty for late standard delivery: **5% credit** on the affected line items.
- Penalty for late emergency delivery: **full refund** of the emergency premium.

## General SLA Terms

### Escalation Matrix

| Severity | Response Time | Contact |
|---|---|---|
| Critical (patient safety risk) | 15 minutes | Supplier emergency hotline |
| High (stock < 24h demand) | 1 hour | Account manager |
| Medium (stock < 48h demand) | 4 hours | Account manager or portal |
| Low (routine reorder) | Next business day | Standard ordering portal |

### Quality Standards

- All suppliers must maintain **ISO 13485** certification for medical device quality management.
- Products must comply with **FDA 21 CFR Part 820** (Quality System Regulation).
- Material Safety Data Sheets (MSDS) must be current and accessible.

### Dispute Resolution

- Billing disputes: resolved within **10 business days**.
- Quality complaints: acknowledged within **24 hours**, root cause analysis within **5 business days**.
